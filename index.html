Conway's Game Of Life

Description:

This project was used to show mastery of the computer science curriculum at this point in the program. Lambda School is a mastery based progression school. Title:

GigaPet

Description:

I was the project lead as well as the head React developer for this project. We worked on a team of 6 developers for two weeks to produce an application that promotes healthy eating in children. Our goal was to create a fun interactive way for parents and children to both learn together about how to eat healthy but in a way that will keep children engaged. During our phase one we implemented food tracking and the ability for a child to pick a monster character for their profile.


In future iterations we discussed possibilities of a few features that would help to engage the children more in learning and motivate them to eat healthy. We discussed implementing a way for the monster character to grow when healthy foods are tracked. Other ideas were also to have either a sad expression for possibly mimicking a stomach ache if too much junk food is tracked.

GitHub Repo: https://github.com/BuildWeek-Gigapet1-2020/front-end

Deploy Link: https://gigapetproject.netlify.app/
